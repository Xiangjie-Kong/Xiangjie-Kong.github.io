## Selected Research Achievement

![](/images/paper1.png)

[Link to paper](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cnsns.2025.108649)

***Two lower boundedness-preservity auxiliary variable methods for a phase-field model of 3D narrow volume reconstruction*** was accepted by ***Communications in Nonlinear Science and Numerical Simulation***, a JCR Q1 top 10% journal. Kong was the first author of the paper.

In this paper, corresponding author **Prof. Junxiang Yang** proposed the numerical method, and **Kong** contributed all numerical tests and paper writing as the first author.
